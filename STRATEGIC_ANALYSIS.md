## 2. The Gaps We Fill (The "Why Now?")

### A. The Cognitive Overload Gap
*   **Reality:** An operator in a control room monitors 20+ screens. When a crisis hits (e.g., Winter Storm Uri), the data flood is overwhelming. Humans cannot process thousands of alarms in seconds.
*   **GridGuard Solution:** The **Agent Orchestrator** reduces 1,000 alarms into 1 strategic "BLUF" (Bottom Line Up Front) recommendation. It reduces cognitive load by 90%.

### B. The Knowledge Gap (The "Silver Tsunami")
*   **Reality:** 50% of utility workforce is retiring in the next 5-10 years. Institutional knowledge is walking out the door.
*   **GridGuard Solution:** The **RAG Knowledge Base** captures historical incident reports and protocols. The AI acts as an "Immortal Mentor" to junior operators, answering questions like "What did we do last time the frequency dropped?"

### C. The Silo Gap
*   **Reality:** The "Trading Desk" (Market) and the "Control Room" (Physics) often don't talk until it's too late.
*   **GridGuard Solution:** The dashboard unifies **LMP Prices** (Market) with **Frequency/Inertia** (Physics) in a single pane of glass, allowing for economic optimization that doesn't compromise safety.
